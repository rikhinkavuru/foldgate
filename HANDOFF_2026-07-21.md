## 1. The paper in one screen

**Thesis.** Co-folding confidence (AF3/Boltz/Chai) correlates with pose accuracy but is not a decision
rule and is weakest on novel pockets/chemotypes. `foldgate` turns frozen confidence into a
risk-controlled accept/abstain gate (Learn-then-Test, exact binomial, α=0.20 δ=0.10), shows the
guarantee **collapses under novelty shift**, proves an **exact score-conditional decomposition** of why
no training-free reweighting can fix it, and **repairs** what is repairable with group-conditional
calibration, pricing the repair honestly.

**Structure (3 contributions, Digital Discovery draft):**
1. The break + the per-stratum **feasibility frontier** (the impossibility, stated across a score family).
2. The **exact decomposition as a deployable drift diagnostic**.
3. The **honestly-priced group-conditional repair** with a loss-matched certifier.

**Data.** Released Runs N' Poses (RNP): 13,535 delivered poses (top-1 by ranking_score), 6 co-folding
models; the 5 governed models (AF3, Boltz-1, Boltz-1x, Chai-1, Protenix) give 12,602 poses / 2,425
systems / 11,254 target-labels. Boltz-2 (933 poses) is an ungoverned affinity comparator, analyzed
separately with its own 2023 reference. Label: correct iff BiSyRMSD ≤ 2 Å. CPU-only tabular; the
structure-feature + D3 arms need the 37 GB `prediction_files.tar.gz` (local) / a GPU.

**Headline numbers (all committed JSON):**
- Deploy-familiar-→-novel realized error **0.55** (AF3, 90% CI [0.52,0.58], n=734; range 0.48–0.66 across
  5 models) vs the 0.20 target. `results/break_money_numbers.json`.
- **Score-family feasibility frontier: 13 of 40** non-reference cells admit no threshold on ANY score
  (ranking 22/40, ipTM 16/40, ligand-pLDDT 18/40) at any coverage (α=0.20); 21/40 at α=0.10.
  `results/e50_score_family_frontier.json`. Robust to min-accept (19–24 across 10–50, `e56`), RMSD cutoff
  (16–35 across 1–3 Å, `e59`), binning (~0.4 fraction across 3/5/7 bins, `e26`).
- **The break is base-rate, not signal-collapse**: within-S3 native-score AUROC 0.71 (AF3)/0.64/0.56,
  all > chance; base correctness on S3 is 0.44. Signal collapses to chance only on S4. `e45`.
- Leakage-free nested-LOTO matched pair: **AF3 combined 73%** at HB-UB 0.192 (4/5 folds) vs native 20%
  (uncertified, HB-UB 0.223, 1/2 folds); Chai/Protenix native abstain. `e34`. Robust to cluster-grouping
  (0.72, `e41`).
- Label cost: median **38** to certify a feasible cell (exact binomial vs 60 HB, 102 Hoeffding); ~**80**
  in-stratum labels to extend one stratum out (combined score); closed form n_g ≥ ⌈ln(1/δ)/KL(r‖α)⌉,
  floor 11. `d2_certify.json`, `e28`, `e52`.

## 4. Durable facts (do not re-derive wrong)

- **The impossibility is a SCORE-FAMILY claim now** (13/40), not ranking_score-only (22/40). A4/D1
  showed the count is score-dependent (better scores lower it), so we report the intersection across
  {ranking, ipTM, ligand-pLDDT mean & min}. **PL-PAE is NOT in the released RNP dump** (CIF + ranking
  only) — the one pose-triage signal we couldn't test; would need regeneration.
- **The break is base-rate-bound, not discrimination-bound** (A5). The score still ranks on novel strata
  (AUROC 0.71); base correctness is just too low to certify α. This is the sharper framing throughout.
- **σ(s) framing** (A1): η_P/η_Q are score-conditional (P(L=1|s)); Δ̄ = accept-region score-fiber gap;
  the invariance has two separate reasons (σ(s)-measurability + coverage-pinning). "Concept shift" was an
  overclaim; use "score-conditional reliability drift."
- **Novelty is worth more as a stratifier than a score feature** (D28): training-free costs only ~0.013
  AURC; the native→combined gain is 0.06–0.12. This validates the whole design (stratify on novelty,
  don't score with it). The combined score IS mildly novelty-correlated (|ρ|≤0.35, e36) → achievability
  side; the impossibility governs the frozen NATIVE score.
- **S4 is mixed** (A3): report it as a diagnostic extreme, lead every claim on S3. ~29/76 genuine core.
- **Sequence + temporal axes are uninformative in RNP for benchmark-redundancy reasons** (D19/e25): 37%
  of systems at the sequence-identity ceiling; RNP is wholly post-2021-cutoff. Not evidence those axes
  are safe — the benchmark can't test them. Boltz-2 (2023 cutoff) is the one genuine temporal boundary
  and shows the same structural break (e55).
- `crcgupta` = **V. Kotte, arXiv:2606.29054** (concurrent independent LLM-domain reweighting-invariant
  floor). We concede concurrency, claim the co-folding instantiation + per-stratum localization.
- Every headline coverage number is **target-grouped (leakage-free)**. Pose-level splits (e4/e9/e11-iid)
  are SI-only. The nested-LOTO protocol (e34) is the headline.
- Certificate cards are labeled a **proposal** (numbers validated, ergonomics not user-tested).
